The impact of COVID-19 on the hospitality industry has been profound, prompting us to engage with numerous operators, both large and small, to discern their post-pandemic educational needs. The feedback was resounding: many new staff members lacked experience and required comprehensive training "from the ground up." This drove us to establish On The Rocks Mixology 101, ensuring every bartender receives the essential tools to navigate their initial shifts successfully. As we move forward, On The Rocks "Advanced Mixology" and "Complete Mixologist" will continually expand, evolving year by year. We encourage you to explore the content at your own pace, and if there's specific content you'd like to see incorporated, don't hesitate to share your input. Moreover, we've taken this opportunity to collaborate with a fresh generation of bartenders, bar owners, and managers to shape the content you encounter today. Our commitment lies in representing the diverse bartender community in all its forms, embodying inclusivity for everyone we serve.
