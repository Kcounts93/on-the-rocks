3 Career Pathways

Through our online, comprehensive course pathways you will become a certified Mixologist!

Course 1: Mixology 101 - Beginners

Description: This course is designed for individuals with no prior bartending experience. Whether you're a cocktail enthusiast or just looking to learn the basics, Mixology 101 is the perfect starting point. Offerings: Cocktail Essentials: Learn the fundamental techniques of mixing and serving popular cocktails, including shaking, stirring, muddling, and garnishing. Ingredients and Tools: Discover essential ingredients, spirits, mixers, and tools used in bartending, and understand how to select quality ingredients for your drinks. Classic Cocktail Recipes: Master a selection of classic cocktails like the Martini, Margarita, and Mojito, and gain a solid foundation in mixing and presentation. Tasting and Pairing: Explore the art of tasting and pairing cocktails with different cuisines, enhancing your ability to create balanced and flavorful drinks. Included: Video lessons with step-by-step demonstrations Printable recipe cards for practice Quizzes and assignments to reinforce learning Access to a community forum for Q&A and interaction

Course 2: Advanced Mixology - Intermediate and Non-Professional Bartenders

Description: Elevate your bartending skills to the next level with Advanced Mixology. Perfect for those with some experience who want to refine their techniques and expand their repertoire. Offerings: Crafting Signature Cocktails: Learn to create unique and innovative cocktails, experimenting with flavor combinations and creative presentation. Mixology Techniques: Dive deep into advanced techniques like infusions, foams, and molecular mixology, adding flair to your creations. Bartending Efficiency: Enhance your speed and efficiency behind the bar with lessons on multitasking, time management, and handling high-volume situations. Customer Experience: Understand the psychology of customer interactions, perfect your customer service skills, and develop strategies for upselling. Included: In-depth video tutorials covering advanced mixology concepts Hands-on assignments to practice creating original cocktails Case studies and real-world scenarios for practical learning Access to a private networking group for peer interaction and mentorship

Course 3: Complete Mixologist - Bartending Certifications and Comprehensive Knowledge

Description: For those seeking a comprehensive education in bartending, from gaining state certifications to becoming a knowledgeable and skilled mixologist. Offerings: Bartending Certification Preparation: Receive expert guidance in preparing for and passing bartending certification exams, ensuring you're fully qualified for professional bartending roles. Mastering Mixology: Delve into the history, science, and art of mixology, studying advanced cocktail families, spirits, and regional variations. Bar Management Fundamentals: Learn the business side of bartending, including inventory management, menu development, pricing strategies, and team leadership. Professional Development: Gain career-building skills such as resume building, interview techniques, and marketing yourself as a professional bartender. Included: Comprehensive course materials covering all aspects of bartending Practice quizzes and mock certification exams Interactive workshops and virtual hands-on sessions Personalized career guidance and job placement support
